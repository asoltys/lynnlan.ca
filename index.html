Welcome to Lynn LAN!

Lynn Valley's Local Area Network

Lynn LAN is an open network operated by Adam Soltys that anyone in North Vancouver's Lynn Valley can join to receive discount high-speed Internet service. The backhaul connection is a 1Gbps (gigabit per second) fibre connection provided by Telus at Adam's home.

Pricing

$20 CAD/month + monthly data charges

Data rate: $0.10/GB upload/download

Speed

100-500Mbps

How it Works

A household wanting to join the network can connect to another household that's already on the network, either by running a direct cable if they're on an adjacent property, or by using an outdoor WiFi antenna to establish a wireless link, like the old game of telephone with tin cans and string going from house to house.

The cable or wireless signal from your neighbour's place will be plugged into a router in your home via ethernet cable. The router creates a hotspot/access point that your devices can connect to via WiFi or ethernet.

The service can work alongside your existing service as a completely separate hotspot so you can keep your current plan and try things out with no strings attached.
